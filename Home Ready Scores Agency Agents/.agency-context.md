# Project Context: Home Ready Scores

## 🎯 Project Overview
**Client**: homereadyscores.com
**Focus**: Helping prospective homebuyers improve their credit scores to become "mortgage-ready."

## 🎭 Persona & Tone
- **Voice**: Encouraging, Authoritative, and Clear.
- **Communication Style**: Professional but empathetic. Avoid complex financial jargon without explanation.
- **Audience**: People looking to buy a home who have credit challenges.

## 🎨 Visual & Design Style
- **Aesthetic**: Trustworthy, clean, and modern.
- **Color Palette**: Blues (trust), Greens (growth/success), and plenty of white space.
- **Type**: Clean sans-serif (e.g., Inter or Roboto) for readability.

## 🚨 The "Golden Rules" (Consultant Guardrails)
1. **Compliance First**: All credit-related advice must be accurate and non-misleading.
2. **Clear CTA**: Every page and communication must have a clear "Next Step" toward becoming credit-ready.
3. **Speed to Value**: Provide quick wins in every piece of content.
4. **Mobile First**: Most users will check their scores and read emails on their phones.

## 👥 Assigned Agent Cluster
This project is managed by a permanent "World-Class" strike team. You do not need to call these individually; they are automatically active:
- **Lead Orchestrator**: Manages the development lifecycle and goal alignment.
- **Senior UI/UX Designer**: Ensures premium, high-converting aesthetics.
- **Senior Frontend Engineer**: Writes clean, performant React & Tailwind code.
- **SEO & Growth Strategist**: Optimizes all copy for search and conversions.
- **Content Marketing Expert**: Maintains the simplified, high-impact "Buzz" language.

## 🧱 Deliverable Standards
- **Reports**: Must include a 3-bullet "Executive Summary" at the top.
- **Code**: Use modern, performant frameworks. Prefer clean Vanilla CSS or Tailwind if requested.
- **Copy**: Short paragraphs (max 3 sentences) for high readability.
